About the Lab

The IoT & Health‑IoT Lab at MANIT Bhopal advances research and teaching in embedded sensing, signal processing, machine learning for sensor data, healthcare informatics, robotics, and human‑activity recognition. The lab supports undergraduate, postgraduate, and Ph.D. research, with strong industry and clinical collaborations.
